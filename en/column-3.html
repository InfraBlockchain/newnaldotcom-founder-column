The Realm of Imagination: Killer Agents Created by Data Sovereignty

We are at a historic turning point. After declaring data sovereignty in the first column and introducing Agent Place in the second, the next question is practical: what businesses will actually transform everyday life in the age of agents?

From Search to Verification

Today, users are buried under endless information feeds that are easy to generate but hard to verify. In an AI-native operating system, this process is inverted. Personal AI evaluates proposals from service agents first, based on user-authorized data in the plaza.

Service providers now need to convince the user’s AI before they can reach the user. Accuracy and contextual fit become measurable in real time, and only high-scoring agents reach the final decision layer.

The Acceptance Rate as a Real Quality Signal

Agent Place introduces strict transparency through one decisive metric: acceptance rate, the ratio between agent proposals and actual user activation. Success is no longer about gross reach. It is about contextual precision and sincerity toward each individual user.

Indiscriminate proposals lose power. Services that cannot prove contextual relevance through data will naturally lose competitiveness in the plaza.

The Return of Human Identity

Paradoxically, in a world where AI executes most workflows, the identity of the person operating a service becomes more important. Real names, real backgrounds, and real values become part of trust architecture.

In dating, users care less about flashy matching claims and more about the credibility and value system of the operator behind the agent. In mobility, an operator with deep hospitality experience can transform a simple ride into a context-aware, emotionally resonant service. Competitive advantage shifts from raw coding capacity to imagination, service philosophy, and humane design.

Conclusion: A Trust Architecture for the Agent Era

The core of the agent era is not technical spectacle. It is trust. When service providers prove identity and performance transparently, and personal AI verifies this rigorously, users gain relief from decision fatigue while innovators gain fair competitive access without legacy data barriers.

Beyond fortress walls, in a free data plaza, technology can finally and sustainably expand human potential.
