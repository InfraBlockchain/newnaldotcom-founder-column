Founder Column 03

The Realm of Imagination: The Era of 'Killer Agents' Created by Data Sovereignty

We are at a historic turning point, tearing down the Data Fortresses and establishing a transparent Digital Town Square. Having declared the reclamation of data sovereignty in the first column and proposed the Agent Place as a new ecosystem beyond the App Store in the second, it is now time to envision what specific businesses will change our lives — the reality of the Killer Agent.

§ 1From Search to Verification: The End of Mindless Scrolling

Today, users live in a flood of information — images and text that are easy to create but impossible to verify. Users must waste energy wandering through infinite scrolls and ads just to find what they need. In the era of AI-native operating systems, this process is completely subverted. Based on the data users open in the Plaza, the Personal AI (My AI) first reads and analyzes proposals from service agents.

Now, service providers must first convince the user's AI. The Personal AI scores the accuracy and suitability of the data provided by the agent in real-time, and only agents with high scores win the user's final decision. This means the user transitions from a "searcher" lost in information to a sovereign authority holder who only exercises final decision-making power over precisely filtered results.

§ 2The Weight of a Proposal: The 'Acceptance Rate' as a Measure of Quality

The Agent Place demands even stricter transparency from service providers than from users. This is because the Acceptance Rate — the ratio of an agent's proposals to actual user executions — is transparently disclosed in the Plaza. This demands a high level of moral tension from service providers. Success is no longer measured by how many people you reach, but by how sophisticated and sincere your proposal is to a single user.

Indiscriminate proposals no longer work. Services that cannot prove through data how well their proposals fit a user's life will naturally be phased out of the competition in the Plaza. Ultimately, the acceptance rate becomes an indicator of how deeply the agent understands the user's context, serving as the most powerful reputation metric directly linked to the survival of the business.

§ 3The Return of Humanity: Philosophy and Story as the Ultimate Business Model

Paradoxically, in an era where everything is performed by AI, the identity of the person operating that service becomes a more critical basis for judgment than the anonymous algorithms hidden behind digital veils. Disclosing the real names and histories of service agents is a declaration that the philosophy, life, and sincerity of the human behind the agent will be proven through data.

In a dating agent, for example, what truly matters is not a flashy matching algorithm, but the reliability of the agent making the introduction. Users open their hearts when they see who the operator is and what values they hold in arranging meetings. The same applies to Ride-hailing Agents. A ride-hailing agent operated by someone who spent a lifetime as a luxury hotel manager will read the user's preferences to pre-arrange a decaf oat latte, play their favorite music, and offer a greeting of unparalleled grace.

Business success now depends not on coding prowess but on the operator's imagination and story — considering how to use user-authorized data to beautifully design human experiences.

ConclusionA New Symbiosis Created by the Architecture of Trust

Ultimately, the core of the Agent era lies not in the splendor of technology but in the trust that flows beneath it. A structure where service providers transparently prove their identity and performance, and where Personal AI rigorously verifies them, offers users liberation from decision fatigue and innovators an opportunity for fair competition without data barriers.

When data flows in the free plaza beyond the fortress walls, technology finally becomes a tool that infinitely extends human potential. This new ecosystem, where transparency equals service quality and human sincerity is the most powerful competitive advantage, will be the most reliable milestone for the AI era we face.
