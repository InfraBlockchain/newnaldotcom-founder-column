The Realm of Imagination: Killer Agents Created by Data Sovereignty

YT Kim, Newnal founder
May 8, 2026

After sovereignty and infrastructure, the next question is execution: what services will matter in real life? In the agent era, users should not search endlessly. Personal AI should verify and filter before users decide.

From Search to Verification

Service providers must convince the user’s AI with quality and contextual fit. Acceptance rate becomes a transparent metric of actual value rather than raw distribution volume.

The Return of Human Trust

As AI execution becomes commoditized, the operator’s philosophy and reliability become critical. In this model, imagination, context design, and human intent become core competitive assets.

Conclusion

The agent era is ultimately a trust architecture. Transparent proof, rigorous verification, and context-centric service design can reduce user fatigue while enabling fair innovation.
