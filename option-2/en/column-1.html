Beyond the Data Fortress: Igniting the Path to the Digital Data Plaza

YT Kim, Newnal founder
May 5, 2026

For decades, digital security has been built around fortress-style central servers. That model protected data, but it also created silos that separated individuals from visibility into how their own data is used.

Newnal proposes a structural shift: data is best protected not by opaque isolation but by verifiable usage under transparent infrastructure.

From Fortress to Plaza

In Newnal’s model, identifying information remains encrypted while usage trails become auditable on public-ledger infrastructure. This changes protection from hidden control to verifiable ownership.

The Newnal Phone and Reverse Login

With AIOS, users no longer surrender data by entering services. Authorized agents perform reverse login to request access to user-controlled, decentralized data infrastructure.

Conclusion

The Data Fortress era is ending. The Digital Data Plaza is a practical path to restoring user sovereignty and making trustworthy AI services possible at scale.
